Exploring Power Signatures for Location Forensics of Media Recordings

IEEE Signal Processing Cup 2016

Extraction of electrical grid fingerprint from audio recordings utilizing electromagnetic interference induced Electrical Network Frequency (ENF) through digital signal processing techniques. Design and implementation of intelligent geolocation classifier system using machine learning. Presented the work at the International Conference on Acoustics, Speech and Signal Processing (ICASSP) 2016 in Shanghai, China. Awarded 1st prize.
